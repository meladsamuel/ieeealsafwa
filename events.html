Makerfire is a global celebration that takes place every year through exhibitions, workshops and activities in many fields, ranging from handicrafts to robotics and three-dimensional printing, through arts and sciences in a way that is entertaining and mainly educational. Through which you can learn everything new about the culture of industrialization through the exhibition of makers and various lectures, and participate yourself in many activities in order to live the experience, this is not technical and scientific presentations throughout the day to take place.
