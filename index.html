Vision

Participate in the development of our society by providing opportunities for our students to enter competitions and scientific conferences in order to enhance students' soft skills and technical expertise “Technology for all, not only engineers".

Mission

Creating a professional environment for the student in IEEE Al-Safwa SB to decrease the gap between academic study and practical life, by providing technical sessions, workshops, and event for helping the students to be updated with all new technologies in all fields of engineering.

This season, the first of us, we aim to create something new and different, develop leadership skills, and commit to serve our communities.
